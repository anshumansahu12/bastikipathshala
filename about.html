Our Mission

At Basti Ki Pathshala, our mission is to uplift underprivileged children and communities by focusing on holistic development. We believe that every child deserves a safe, healthy, and educated future.

  • Free Education: Provide basic education and tutoring to children living in slums and streets.
  • Nutrition Support: Distribute healthy meals to ensure no child studies on an empty stomach.
  • Skill Development: Equip children with life skills and vocational training for a better tomorrow.
  • Healthcare Camps: Organize free medical check-ups and awareness drives for families.
